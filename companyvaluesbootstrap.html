Our Values

We believe in the value of taking risks. Facebook has always and will continue to welcome bold problem-solvers with ideas that push boundaries and break conventions. As we like to say at Facebook: “The riskiest thing is to take no risks.” If we can, we should.



The security of your personal information and your safety offline are our highest priorities. We are constantly working to stay one step ahead of the world’s most advanced cybersecurity threats. We design and build products with an eye to safety, always exercising the utmost caution.

Block out the noise. At Facebook, we want to give you the power to see more of what you want to see, whether that’s a new video by your favourite artist or the latest deals from your favourite brand. We help you to customize your feed to create the optimal experience.

Exposure to different viewpoints makes us all stronger. It is our mission at Facebook to bring the world closer together by facilitating conversations that cross divides. Understanding each other starts with hearing each other.

We are committed to building a platform for the expression of opinions that represent the full spectrum of our community’s diversity.

We are committed to providing everyone, regardless of identity or creed, a say. We give you the power to participate in whatever conversations you like however you like. We firmly believe: “Your Facebook, your voice.”
